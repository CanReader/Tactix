Detailed Description

Fixed-capacity squad: a roster of agent handles with roles, slots, a leader and a current tactic.

The squad is pure bookkeeping. It tracks who is in the group, what role each one plays, which formation slot they own, who leads, and what the group is currently trying to do. It deliberately does no movement or decision-making itself; the formation system reads slot indices from here, and higher-level AI reads/sets the tactic.
