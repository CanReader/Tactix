Detailed Description

Small, engine-agnostic vector and scalar math used across Tactix.

These types are deliberately not UE's FVector. Keeping a self-contained Tactix::FTactixVec3 is what lets TactixCore (and TactixSystems on top of it) compile and unit-test with no Unreal dependency at all. The conversions between FVector and these types live in TactixUE, where the engine is already in scope; they have no place down here.

Nothing fancy in the implementations, just the operations the AI systems actually use: dot, cross, length, normalise, distance, and a handful of scalar helpers.
